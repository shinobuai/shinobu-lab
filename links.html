GENESIS MD software

GENESIS is a high-performance molecular dynamics simulation software developed in Japan, designed for large-scale biomolecular simulations on modern supercomputing architectures. It supports atomistic and coarse-grained models, enhanced sampling methods, and free-energy calculations.

BioSim Talks

A monthly English-language seminar series in computational biophysics held in U. Osaka and online. It welcomes a broad audience, from students to researchers across diverse scientific areas.
